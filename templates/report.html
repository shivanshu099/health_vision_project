Weekly Workout & Meal Plan - Rai

Day Workout Meals Notes
Monday

Cardio

30 minutes of brisk walking

Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and nuts

Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens

Dinner: Baked salmon with roasted vegetables

Focus on portion control and healthy fats.
Tuesday

Strength Training

Full-body workout with bodyweight exercises (squats, push-ups, lunges)

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with fruit and granola

Lunch: Lentil soup with whole-grain bread

Dinner: Chicken stir-fry with brown rice

Hydrate well throughout the day.
Wednesday

Rest

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with whole-wheat toast

Lunch: Turkey sandwich on whole-wheat bread

Dinner: Vegetarian chili with cornbread

Focus on getting enough sleep.
Thursday

Cardio

30 minutes of swimming or cycling

Breakfast: Smoothie with fruit, yogurt, and protein powder

Lunch: Quinoa salad with black beans and veggies

Dinner: Baked chicken breast with sweet potato fries

Listen to your body and take breaks as needed.
Friday

Strength Training

Focus on lower body with squats, lunges, and hamstring curls

Breakfast: Whole-wheat pancakes with fruit and syrup

Lunch: Tuna salad on whole-wheat bread

Dinner: Pizza with whole-wheat crust and lots of veggies

Try to incorporate healthy fats into your diet.
Saturday

Active Rest

Go for a hike or play a sport

Breakfast: Waffles with fruit and whipped cream

Lunch: Burgers with a side salad

Dinner: Tacos with lean ground beef and all the fixings

Enjoy a cheat meal but don't go overboard.
Sunday

Rest

Breakfast: Eggs Benedict with avocado toast

Lunch: Leftovers from Saturday

Dinner: Roasted chicken with mashed potatoes and gravy

Get enough sleep and prepare for the week ahead.
